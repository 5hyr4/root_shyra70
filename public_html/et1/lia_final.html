For the LIA, I decided to combine Emerging Tech and Design 1: History & Principles. I plan on creating a short animation of the superhero character I made for Design: History & Principles using Blender. I will begin by making a low-poly character using only polygons and materials to demonstrate what we've learned in class. Then, to challenge myself, I will be animating the character performing the action of running along with animating a few cameras. Finally, I will create a background of a few buildings and add some warm/green lighting to set the scene.
