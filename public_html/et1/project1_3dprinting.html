DESCRIPTION: For this project, I decided to create a 2 in 1 birthday present for my mom; a birthday card and a picture frame. The card/frame will be able to open like a book with a hinge between the two pages. On one page, I will include flowers as a design to make it prettier and on the second page there will be a page to put a picture inside. The dimensions will be about 10 cm X 15 cm since it's the standard size for a developped photo and it's also the size of the photo I would like to put inside.
