SOLUTION

HOW?

In order to come up with the characters, I took into consideration two factors:

  • The target audience

Since our game is targeted towards younger people, mostly teenagers, I wanted to create characters that resemebled them. Additionally, I chose a cuter and cozier artstyle to match the indie aesthetic of the game.

  • Characteristics

In order to figure out each of their physical appearances, I decied to base them off of a single word characteristic they might have. For example, the first character is defined as spontaneous, the second character is strong and the third character is smart. This is also a way to differentiate, and give each of them a unique vibe.

COLOR CHOICES

This character is our main character so I wanted to make him stand out with the colors I used, mainly the color GREEN since it ties in with the topic of the environment. In order to portray his spontaneous, bright and optimistic personality, I chose the color YELLOW for his hair and ORANGE for his scarf.

This character's main characteristic is his strength. Therefore, I thought it was appropriate to give him darker colors like BLACK and BROWN. Also, to show courage and power, I made his shiirt the color RED.

This character's main characteristic is her intelligence so I decided to make her colors mainly BLUE. I added accent colors like YELLOW and RED to represent her optimism and perseverance to find solutions
