BLAST MINI

ABOUT US

In the heartbeat of a city that never stops moving, a new sound breaks through, bold, clean, and unapologetically expressive. Born from the spirit of street art, this speaker fuses raw creative energy with sleek minimalism. Pops of color cut through sharp lines like spray paint on concrete, turning every beat into a brushstroke of personality. It slips into your hand, but fills the room, made to move, made to be seen, made to be heard. This isn’t just about music. It’s about making noise, making statements, and owning your vibe wherever you go. Your sound. Your canvas. Your story.

Our design is inspired by 1970s graffiti era and minimalism, two completely different styles. While the value is found inside inside the box with our Blast Mini speaker, we believe in the importance of a story and experience, which we included in the design of our packaging. We stuck with simple colors in order to portray the minimalism with a neon green that stands out. The main element of the outer box is the brick wall that's used to illustrate the speaker's sound breaking through it. The inner-box conveys the loudness in its design with the elements of graffiti and the neon green color. Hence, it all ties in with the expressing oneself with their own unique sound.

THE SPEAKER

Blast Mini offers quality bluetooth speakers. They are small in size and big in sound, excellent for every day use. A compact speaker that will last all day so you can bring your sound everywhere you go.
